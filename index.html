核心句式
The figure for X rose sharply from A to B between [year] and [year].
X 的数值从…急剧上升至…
X accounted for the largest proportion, at approximately N%.
X 占比最大，约为…%
After peaking at N in [year], the number gradually declined.
在…年达到峰值后，数量逐渐下降
Overall, it is clear that X saw the most notable change over the period.
总体而言，X 变化最为显著

尽管初期有所上升，X 随后回落至原始水平
Task 1 · 地图 / 流程图类
地图题 · 位置方位词
to the north/south/east/west of in the centre / on the outskirts adjacent to / next to / opposite along the river / beside the road between X and Y
地图题 · 变化动词
be replaced by / give way to be converted into / be transformed into be demolished / be removed be constructed / be built / be added be extended / be expanded / be widened remain unchanged / retain its original position
流程图 · 顺序连接词
first / initially / to begin with then / next / after that / subsequently at this stage / at the following stage once X has been done, Y is then... finally / ultimately / at the end of the process
核心句式
The most significant change is that X has been replaced by Y.
最显著的变化是 X 被 Y 所取代
What is particularly noticeable is that the area to the north has been substantially developed.
尤为引人注目的是北部区域得到了大规模开发
The raw material is first collected and then transported to the factory, where it undergoes processing.
原材料首先被采集，然后运往工厂进行加工
Overall, the maps show a dramatic transformation of the area from largely rural to predominantly urban.
总体而言，地图呈现了该地区从农村向城市的剧变
Task 2 · 论点论证
引入观点
It is argued that... Proponents of X contend that... A growing body of evidence suggests that... One of the main reasons for this is that...
让步与转折
While / Although / Even though Admittedly, ... however, ... Despite this, / Nevertheless, On the other hand, The counter-argument is that...
举例说明
For example, / For instance, To illustrate this point, A case in point is... Take X as an example. This is evident in / This can be seen in...
因果关系
This leads to / This results in / This causes As a result, / Consequently, / Therefore, due to / owing to / as a consequence of contribute to / give rise to / bring about
论证核心句式
The primary reason for this is that X directly leads to Y, which in turn causes Z.
主要原因在于 X 直接导致 Y，进而引发 Z
Admittedly, there are merits to this view; however, the drawbacks far outweigh the benefits.
诚然此观点有其合理之处，但弊端远大于益处
Unless governments take decisive action, the problem is likely to worsen significantly.
除非政府采取果断行动，问题将会显著恶化
While this may be true in certain contexts, it does not apply universally.
尽管在特定情境下可能成立，但并非普遍适用
Task 2 · 结构句式
开头段 · 引入改写题目
It is widely believed that... / In recent years, ... has become a growing concern.
引入话题背景
This essay will argue that... / This essay will examine both sides of the argument before reaching a conclusion.
亮明立场 / 说明文章结构
主体段 · 段首主题句
One significant advantage of X is that...
优势段开头
However, there are several compelling arguments against this view.
反驳段开头
From a social / economic / environmental perspective, ...
分角度论证
The most effective solution to this problem would be to...
解决方案段开头
结尾段
In conclusion, while X has some merit, I firmly believe that Y is the more persuasive argument.
表明立场式结尾
To conclude, both individuals and governments must work together to address this issue effectively.
呼吁行动式结尾
